Biography

She is a Chanakya Ph.D. Fellow Awardee sponsored by DRISHTI Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IITIndore). She is pursuing Ph.D. under the supervision of
Dr. Shashank Gupta, Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science and Information Systems, BITS Pilani. Her research interests are majorly in Internet of Vehicles (IoV), Edge computing and Computer Vision in Autonomous Vehicles. She is a student member of IEEE, a student member of ACM , IEEE ComSoc and IEEE CTSoc. She is an active reviewer of many top-tier journals, transactions and conferences. She has also received a competitive International Travel Support (ITS) Grant from DST, India for presenting a manuscript in South Korea in the broad domain of Intelligent Transportation Systems.
